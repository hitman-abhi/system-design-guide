This section builds on the foundational material in `Basics` and focuses on how distributed systems are composed, protected, and operated under realistic production constraints.

These topics usually deal with:

- coordination across services and replicas
- system behavior during overload or failure
- patterns that trade simplicity for scale, resilience, or flexibility
